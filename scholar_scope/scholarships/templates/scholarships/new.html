      Applications for the 2026 edition of the Government of Slovak Republic National Scholarship Programme are now being accepted from interested international candidates.

      We are on twitter, follow us to connect with us - @scholarsregion

      — Scholarship Region (@scholarsregion) January 13, 2022

      The Government of Slovak Republic National Scholarship Program offers fully-funded study opportunities for international students. These scholarships are available for Masters and PhD studies covering up to 1,470 monthly.

      This post is a detailed insight into the details of the scholarship opportunity and how to apply for it.

      Government of Slovak Republic National Scholarship Summary

      • Host Country: Slovakia
      • Study Abroad: Study in Europe
      • Category: Postgraduate Scholarships | Masters Scholarships | PhD Scholarships
      • Eligible Countries: All Countries
      • Reward: Full Scholarship | 620 – 1,470 Monthly Living Allowance | Accommodation | Travel allowance
      • Deadline: October 31, 2025
      • Scholarship Alerts: To receive Scholarship Alerts on WhatsApp, Click HERE

      Government of Slovak Republic National Scholarship Details:

      The National Scholarship Programme of the Slovak Republic (NSP) is financed by the Ministry of Education, Science, Research and Sport of the Slovak Republic from the state budget of the Slovak Republic.

      The provision of government scholarships of the Slovak Republic is a long-term part of the development cooperation of the Slovak Republic.

      In 2026, the Ministry of Education, Research, Development and Youth of the Slovak Republic will implement bilateral development cooperation through the government scholarship program

      Eligibility Requirements for Government of Slovak Republic National Scholarship Program 2026

      To be considered for the 2026 Government of Slovak Republic National Scholarship Program, you must:

      • Be a citizen of a foreign country

      • Be students who:

        • are university students at universities outside Slovakia;
        • are students of the second level of higher education (master’s students), or are students who at the time of the application deadline have already completed at least 2.5 years of their university studies in the same/similar study programme;
        • will be on a study stay in Slovakia during their higher education outside Slovakia and who will be accepted by a public, private, or state higher education institution in Slovakia for academic mobility to study in Slovakia.
      • Be enrolling to study a postgraduate degree in a Slovakian institution
      • Be PhD students whose higher education or scientific training takes place outside Slovakia and who are accepted by a public, private or state higher education institution or a research institution in Slovakia eligible to carry out a doctoral study programme.
      • Be international university teachers, researchers and artists who are invited to a teaching/research/artistic stay in Slovakia by an institution with a valid certificate of eligibility to carry out research and development, which is not a business company.
      • Meet the Language requirements of the country (English, French, Slovak or Spanish)

      Benefits of 2026 Government of Slovak Republic National Scholarship Program:

      • The scholarship is intended to cover international scholarship holders living costs, i.e. the costs related to staying in Slovakia (food, accommodation, etc.), during their study, research/artistic or teaching stay at universities, and in research organizations in Slovakia.
      • The scholarship holder can ask for assistance concerning accommodation and formalities related to entering and staying in the territory of the Slovak Republic either his/her host institution, or he/she can handle all the necessities him-/herself.
      • Amount of the monthly scholarship
        • university students of the second level of higher education 620
        • PhD student 1025
        • university teacher/researcher/artist: 1470

      Required Documents for Government of Slovakia Scholarship Program:

      Required documents that must be attached to online application as listed below:

      • curriculum vitae;
      • motivation letter;
      • detailed study programme
      • two recommendation letters issued by applicants university teachers
      • confirmation issued by the applicants home university confirming that the applicant is a regular student of the respective university at the time of applying
      • scan of applicants bachelors diploma, diploma supplement and state examination certificate (if applicable);
      • admission/invitation letter issued by applicants host university in Slovakia indicating the period of stay.
